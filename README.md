# GreenKnife Downloader

A lightweight background downloader that automatically detects media links from your clipboard and downloads them in the highest available quality.

---

## ✨ Features

### 📋 Smart Clipboard
Simply copy a link from YouTube, TikTok, X (Twitter), Instagram, and many other supported websites. GreenKnife instantly detects it and opens the download window.

### 🌐 Wide Website Support
Powered by **yt-dlp**, allowing downloads from hundreds of supported websites with excellent compatibility.

### ⚡ High Quality Downloads
Downloads the highest available video or audio quality whenever possible.

### 🖥️ Runs Quietly
Stays in your system tray and only appears when needed, keeping your desktop clean.

### 🔔 Notifications
Receive a notification once your download has finished.
